other hives of companies on the lille metropolis

There are different digital environments in addition to incubators such as corporate hives, corporate hothouses and activity areas. Incubators are support structures for very young company creation projects. Corporate Hives is a business that provides beginner companies with physical and relational tools for growth. Corporate hothouses start-up companies with financial and structural support. The areas correspond to the viable business location.

Blanchemaille by Euratechnologies

Blanchemaille is a subsidiary of euratechnologie dedicated to e-commerce in Roubaix. On is located near the major players of e-commerce Private Showroom, OVH and many other . The e-commerce companies extend over 13000m² with a school of trade.

Adrress : 87 rue du Fontenoy 59100 Roubaix

Plaine images

Plain Image is a complete ecosystem on the creative industry in Tourcoing. Participating companies in this space are in the game, audio-visual, virtual reality or design. The big companies are Ankama, Wakanim, Cream french ... There are 125 companies on 40 000m² with 3 schools.

Adrress : 99 A boulevard Constantin Descat 59200 Tourcoing

ruche d'entreprise

Corporate hives welcome companies in creation and evolution. There are companies with diverse and various projects. In Lille metropolis there are 4 hives located in Hellemmes, Villeneuve d'Ascq, Tourcoing and Armentieres.

Cre'innov

Cre'innov is an incubator in relation with the University of Lille. The business district of Villeneuve d'Asq is located close to all its businesses as well as the corporate hive.

Adrress : 11 rue de l’Harmonie 59650 Villeneuve d’ascq

Serre numerique

The digital greenhouse in Valenciennes is an ecosystem on the professions of image and digital creation. There are 17,000m² with 3 schools.

Adrress : 2 rue Peclet 59300 Valenciennes
